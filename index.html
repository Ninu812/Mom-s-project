Favourite place to travel- Hill Stations

Hill Stations

A hill station is a town located at a higher elevation than the nearby plain or valley. Hundreds of hill stations are located in India. Nandi Hills is a hill station in Karnataka which was developed by Ganga Dynasty. In India, which has the largest number of hill stations, most are situated at an altitude of approximately 1,000 to 2,500 metres.



Favourite Books

Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by J. K. Rowling. The novels describe the life of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of "THE SCHOOL OF WICTHCRAFT AND WIZARDRY". The main story arc concerns Harry's struggle against Lord Voldemort, a dark wizard who intends to become immortal.

Roald Dahl's

Dahl's short stories are known for their unexpected endings, and his children's books for their unsentimental, often darkly comic mood, featuring villainous adult enemies of the child characters. His works include James and the Giant Peach, Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, Matilda, The Witches, Fantastic Mr Fox, The BFG, The Twits, The Giraffe, the Pelly and Me and George's Marvellous Medicine and Tales of the Unexpected.

The Secret Series

The Secret Series is a series of adventure novels written by Enid Blyton. There are six books, as follows:
The Secret Island
The Secret of Spiggy Holes
The Secret Mountain
The Secret of Killimooin (Retitled as The Secret Forest)
